guidelines + policies



Know before you go > IMPORTANT<< /h1>

Lush Skin Studio is located inside of Cascade Executive Suites located at 237 NE Chaklov Dr Vancouver Wa 98684

NOTE that I am a solo esthetician meaning I have a single office suite, if someone else opens the main door for you please wait in the lobby until I come get you, don’t come into the suite unless directed to do so for respect and privacy of other guests that may be getting a service before you.

The main doors are locked at all times so I will need to come get you, when you arrive please text me (preferred) if you call and I don’t pick up, it is because I am still with another client. You may wait outside the building or in your car until I am ready for you.

You can park in the front, or back of the building, there are entrances at both, just text me which entrance you are at so I can get you at the right place. This will be the protocol for every appointment. (Parking is free :)

I accept cash, but I do not carry change with me so it must be the exact amount :)

Before your appointment

Be sure to arrive at least 10 minutes early if it is your first appointment with Lush Skin Studio as you must fill out consent forms to get serviced.

Come with no makeup on if possible, if makeup is on it will be removed by the esthetician.

Skip any strong at-home facial treatments the week before your appointment (actives such as retinols/retinoids, acids, etc) and any exfoliation treatments a few days prior.

Try to stay out of the sun (and always use sunscreen) and away from tanning beds for five days before and after your facial.

If you need to pluck/shave facial hairs, please do so at least 24 hours before your appointment. This only pertains to you if your appointment is a facial/chemical peel/dermaplaning.

Work out before coming to your appointment, rather than after (if possible; You don’t want to sweat on your freshly cleansed face).

Plan extra time for finding parking.

Children and guest policy

Due to safety considerations and limited space, children are not permitted in the salon (unless they are getting the service). Please make prior arrangements for your children before arriving to your appointment

If you must bring an adult guest with you, they must wait in the lobby for you during your appointment.

cancellation/rescheduling policies

The card required to book is not a deposit, your card will not be charged upon booking. If you do a no-call-no-show or don’t cancel/reschedule at least 12 hours before your appointment time, you will be charged 50% of the total service cost to the card on file. Service charges are non-refundable. *You can reschedule or cancel your appointment in the email confirmation that you receive after booking or call/text (360)558-8370 (please leave a voicemail if there is no answer to your call).

Late policy

Any client arriving 15 minutes late to our scheduled appointment time will be accommodated as much as possible but if there is a client right after them, the service will either be cut short or we will have to reschedule (depending on the service(s) booked).

If the client arrives 15 minutes late and I can’t accommodate the client, they will be charged for 50% of their total appointment cost to the card on file (unless you want to change it to another card or cash).

Gift Card Policy

Gift Cards are non-refundable and have no expiration date.

Groupon Policy

Only 2 vouchers for any/all services will be allowed and accepted per person through Groupon. If you already used up your 2 vouchers and book again using Groupon, your appointment and voucher will get canceled and refunded. You can make your appointment directly with me at lushskinstudio.square.site
